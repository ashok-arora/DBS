Introduction

During College it’s tough to manage time, attend all classes and complete all assignments. It is difficult for faculty to manage attendance and marks of all the students of all different batches. It is also equally tough for students too to manage all the different classes and their assignments. Time-table changes from time to time, all the extra classes assigned, and other such information is transmitted through a message which gets lost and causes chaos.

This Management system serves as a medium to solve the above problems and make college life easier for both students and faculty. The marks of each student for each subject will be stored and students will be recommended about how much they need to score to pass. Similarly, the attendance of each student will also be shown graphically with information about how much classes they need to attend to get to the bare minimum. Dynamic Time-Table is also given with changes regarding extra classes reflected on that only.

With the intuitive User-Interface, this system can be used easily by everyone. The password is stored using a combination of hashing and salting encryption system, thus, keeping each user’s information safe. All data is stored in a cloud-based server, so information can be accessed from any system further removing the hassle.


Overview

This Management System will be able to provide an interface:
  • for faculty to mark attendance of every student for a particular subject.
  • for faculty to assign homework and their respective due dates.
  • for faculty to view their day’s hectic time-table for each day.
  • for faculty to add respective lab (mid-sem | major) and theory (minor1 | minor2 | major) grades for each student.
  • for faculty to view each student’s progress throughout the semester.
  • for administrators to modify details about a particular faculty, student or club.
  • for super administrators to modify any part of the database thereby giving them complete control over the database.
  • for various club presidents and secretaries to add new club members to the database.
  • for research assistants and research proposers to view details regarding their project.
  • for students to view their attendance for a particular subject.
  • for students to check their assigned homework and respective due dates.
  • for students to check up-coming club events.
  • for students to keep themselves updated with college news.
